Jorge Rivera-Herrans

Raised in Dorado, Puerto Rico, Jorge Miguel Rivera-Herrans
(nicknamed "Jay") is an artist dedicated to perfecting the craft of
storytelling. His non-traditional approach to songwriting, which brings
includes pulling inspiration from seemingly dissonant mediums,
allows him to create works of art for any audience.

Social Media


Jorge has cultivated notable online followings and communities
of 410,000 on TikTok, 15,000 on Discord, and more across other brands.
platforms.

His goals

Jorge's greatest inspirations come from video games and animation.
In his stories, he aims to emulate the addictive sense of progression
often seen in video games. He aims to write stories with larger than
life themes and characters.

His current project is the upcoming Odyssey-inspired musical EPIC.
